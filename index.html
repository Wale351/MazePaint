HOW TO PLAY

🕹️

Press an arrow key or swipe to launch the ball in that direction.

🎨

The ball slides and paints every tile it passes over until it hits a wall or obstacle.

✅

Fill every tile to complete the level and advance.

🧱

Dark obstacles stop the ball — plan your slides carefully.

📈

Higher difficulty = larger boards and more obstacles. Good luck!
